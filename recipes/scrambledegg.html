Ingredients

  • Butter
  • 6-12 Large Eggs
  • skillet

Steps

  1. Heat skillet on high
  2. Whisk 6-12 eggs until foamy
  3. Melt a 1/8th stick of butter in pan
  4. Pour all of the eggs into pan
  5. Scrape bottom and sides with rubber spatula repeatedly for 2 minutes
  6. Turn off heat and keep scraping bottom and sides of pan
  7. Serve eggs when they have reached desired conistency
  8. Salt and pepper to taste
