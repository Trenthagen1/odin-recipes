Lasagna

Lasagna is a timeless dish for both everyday and special occasions. This recipe is sure to please.

  • 30 minute prep time
  • 45 minute cook time
  • Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • two table spoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • basil leaves to taste
  • italian seasoning
  • lasagna noodles
  • ricotta cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. Brown you meat and add seasonings
  3. Layer your pasta starting with a tomato base on the bottom of the pan and contiue in the following order. Pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce
  4. Cover the last layer with cheese and sauce
  5. Bake for 45 minutes and enjoy
