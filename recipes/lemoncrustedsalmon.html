Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Olive Oil
  • Panko
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Parsley
  • Dill
  • Lemon

  1. Preheat pan
  2. Brush mustard onto salmon
  3. Mix panko with italian seasoning and lemon zest
  4. Bread each salmon filet
  5. On medium heat add olive oil and sear the salmon
  6. Allow salmon to cook over medium heat until 135 is reached
  7. Garnish with lemon wedges and parsley
